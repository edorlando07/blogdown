My First Post: Gratitude and Motivation

2020-05-10

First, I want to give a shout out to the individuals I grind it out every day with at White Lodging. The team leader Adam Hutsler and BI Manager Max Thomas are always coming up with creative ways to solve complex problems and create business value using Tableau and R.

I was also inspired to create this data science blog based on the work performed by Matt Dancho. His online learning platform Business Science University (BSU) walks you through how professional data scientists set up, build, and deploy bleeding edge machine learning models using R. I have completed the following BSU courses and will officially publish some projects in the upcoming weeks.

  • 101-R: Data Science Foundations
  • 102-R: Web Applications
  • 201-R: Advanced Machine Learning

In addition, another BSU student, Jason Dexter, recently created his blog which gave me motivation to formally document some of my own personal projects. His blog can be viewed here.

Lastly, I also want to thank my grad school professors at Valparaiso University including Sonja Streuber, Karl Schmitt, and James Caristi. Grad school provided a great foundation that helped me learn the math and theory behind various modeling techniques. They also introduced me to various software platforms including R and continue to be a valuable resource.

This blog will help me organize my data science projects as well as sharpen my communication skills. Stay tuned for more posts and analysis.
